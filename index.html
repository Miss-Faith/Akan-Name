Akan Name Generator

This web application calculates the day of the week you were born based on your birth details and then depending on your gender, outputs your Akan Name. Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. The Akan people of Ghana frequently name their children the name that corresponds to the day of the week that they were born.

To find out your Akan name; fill the form below and click the get Akan Name button.
